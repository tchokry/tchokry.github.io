About Me

Home / About

Hi, nice to meet you, I'm Tiffany Chokry

I'm a UI/UX Designer with a Computer Science background whose passion lies in creating usable digital products and experiences that leave a positive influence on people, society, and the environment.

Currently, I'm pursuing an undergraduate degree in Design & Interaction with a minor in Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego.

I wholeheartedly believe that design is not merely about art and aesthetics, but more so on problem-solving. As a designer, I aim to build an interface that functions well with its users in mind as my approach to design is often user-user-centric, holistic, and detailed.

When I'm not doing my design work, you can find me knitting, baking, or most often time than not, watching some true-crime series.
